Eacape the cave

  • High Concept: Because immersing in reading a hint of books, a bookworm was lost in the dark cave and want to back home.
  • Theme: Because immersing in reading a hint of books, a bookworm was lost in the dark cave and want to back home.
  • Mandated Variety: This game has keyboard input in order to control the two heroes. Player is 'w', 'a', 's', 'd'.
  • Player Experience Goals: This game tries to make players taste the experience to become a nerd who is lost in the dark forest and want to go back home. Because it is night now and the nerd just can see the environment nearby him.
  • Gameplay: Player1 is 'w', 'a', 's', 'd' while player2 is "up", "down", "left", "right". They need to find keys to open the doors.
  • Prototype Goal: Get out of the cave as soom as possible.
  • Features: The basic idea is the player only can see the environment around him and the player need to remember the road including where are the keys and locked doors.
  • Setting/Premise: The story is abstract.
  • Story: A bookworm was lost in a cave and he want to get out of it. But it is hard for him to see the environment around him because he got thick glasses.
  • Target Audience: Everyone can play this game.
  • Play Time: About 2 minutes.
  • Strategies: It is easy if the player can remember the roads that he / she walked through. Then think about the all the map in mind so that the player can find the keys to open the door. Beating the animals makes the vision bigger so that is helpful to win the game.
