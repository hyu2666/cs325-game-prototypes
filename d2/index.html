The Road to Revenge

  • High Concept: A final fight between the price and the Moster.
  • Theme: The story is adapted from Hamlet, which a monster kill the king who is the father of the little prince. The prince practice for many years and now it is the time...
  • Mandated Variety: There are 4 inputs from user: up: jump left: go left right: go right space: shooting And the game style is arcade game fight.
  • Player Experience Goals: I hope they feel like they enter the game world and they want to retake the crown. Also, it is great if they can enjoy gloomy forest with the map and the bgm.
  • Gameplay: There are 4 inputs from user: up: jump left: go left right: go right space: shooting
  • Prototype Goal: There are two rooms in the game. First one, player should play chess with intelligence. In the second room, player should use his ability to kill the monster in order to retake the crown and save the people of the country.
  • Features: The map and the scenery is elegant and they fit the "revenge" theme.
