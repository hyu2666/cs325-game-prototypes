Clean the Frozen Lake

  • High Concept: A girl wants to clean all the trash from the frozen Mason Pond.
  • Theme: It is the game that adapts from the real George Mason Universy that the student want to clean all the trash on the frozen Mason Pond.
  • Mandated Variety: This game has keyboard input in order to control the movement of player and the shooting of the water pistol. Also, mose input is used to drag the trash into the trash bin.
  • Player Experience Goals: I hope they can undersdand the difficult to coleect all the trash from the pond and they can throw the trash into trash bin.
  • Gameplay: Control: up: go up. left: go left. right: go right. down: go down. space: shoot water. A: make left rotation of water pistol. D: make right rotation of water pistol.
  • Prototype Goal: Clean all the trash to win.
  • Features: The game is adapts from the real Mason campus.
  • Setting/Premise: It is interestin because it happens on campus, wchich is Mason Pond. And the player could be any student in Mason. Although the game is abstraci, I also think some cleaner also do the clean work for the Mason Pond.
  • Story: The Mason Pond has been polluted! And all the canada goose went away. We need to clean the pond and welcome goose come back!
  • Target Audience: Everyone can play this game.
  • Play Time: About 2 minutes.
  • Strategies: Practise to control the moving water pistol to hit the trash to the otherside in order to clean them all.
