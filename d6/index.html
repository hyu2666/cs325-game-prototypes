Invincible Heroes

  • High Concept: The two heroes try to collect gold as much as possible.
  • Theme: The game is designed by myself and I tried to add some skills into the game.
  • Mandated Variety: This game has keyboard input in order to control the two heroes. Player1 is 'w', 'a', 's', 'd' while player2 is "up", "down", "left", "right". And they need to use mouse to click the "start" button to decide how long they can run.
  • Player Experience Goals: I hope they can undersdand the difficult to hit the time bar in order to get to the maximum time. Also, enjoy the Chinese backgruond and background music.
  • Gameplay: Player1 is 'w', 'a', 's', 'd' while player2 is "up", "down", "left", "right". And they need to use mouse to click the "start" button to decide how long they can run. And when a player finish, click End Turn to let the other player to play.
  • Prototype Goal: Get as much gold as possible.
  • Features: The game was designed by myself. And I like the Chinese music and background view.
  • Setting/Premise: The story is abstract.
  • Story: Two European came to China because of unknown force and they need to collect enough money to go back home.
  • Target Audience: Everyone can play this game.
  • Play Time: About 2 minutes.
  • Strategies: Practise to control the time bar. And if you click the button when the pointer goes to the buttom of the bar, you get the longest time.
